Marinya Arwibi Local Water System.

"Welcome to Marinya Arwibi Local Water System. As your elected Chairman, I am honored to serve our community and ensure a reliable, clean, and sustainable water supply for all. Our dedicated team is committed to improving service delivery, addressing challenges, maintaining transparency in our operations, and adopting innovative solutions for better efficiency.

Your support, through timely bill payments, conservation efforts, and valuable feedback, plays a crucial role in strengthening our water system. Together, we can continue to build a better, more efficient, and resilient service for everyone.

Thank you for your trust, cooperation, and commitment to our shared future."
