Vision

To be a leading community based water service provider in Meru region

Mission

To strengthen the enabling environment that build sustainable clean and safe water by leveraging sustainable financial resources and empowering our members to utilize water resource efficiently.

Core Values

The following values define what KKWP stands for and forms the basis on which both the management, employees and other stakeholders work and make decisions on:

1. Innovation and creativity: We will strive to embrace new methods, structures, products, services to serve our customers better.

2. Collaboration: Improving and sustaining performance depends on everyone in the system working as a team.

3. Empowerment: There must be trust on all sides and at all levels for people to improve performance in a sustainable way.

4. Social Responsibility: We value integrity and the promotion of the just use of resources entrusted to us for the enhancement of human life.

5. Community Service: We value a work climate of mutual trust and harmony to accommodate diversity, collaboration and the fulfilment of human potential.

6. Team work: We value shared responsibilities and a collaborative approach on all issues

Core Value statements

An empowered firm is accountable and responsible to its members

Competitive Approach

We compare favorably in terms of performance, service delivery to our customers and the community we serve, with other well performing organizations in the region.

• Management, development and conservation of water and sanitation resources.
• Promotion of water development.
• Water Safety and Quality Assurance.
• Promotion and regulation of water and sanitation, value addition and conservation

Non-Core functions

• Promotion of recreational water facilities.
• Promotion of recreational water facilities.

• Promotion of affordable and safe drinking packaged water.

Policy Priorities

• Strengthening of institutional framework and sustainable funding.
• Sustainable utilization of water resources.
• Achievement of efficient and effective water and sanitation management.
• Promotion of sustainable and efficient sanitation.
• Promotion of sustainable and efficient recreational and ornamental water resources.
• Promotion of water quality and safety, consumption, trade and investment.
• Improvement of infrastructure and human resource development.
• Promotion and coordination of water and sanitation research.
• Enhancement of water and sanitation information and extension service.
• Promotion of regional and international cooperation
