KATHITA KIIRUA WATER PROJECT

Welcome to Kathita Kiirua Water Project

Nestled in the serene community of Kiirua, Kathita Kiirua Water Project is a lifeline to thousands, supplying clean, treated, and ready-to-drink water across Kiirua and its neighboring areas. Powered by the vibrant spirit of community leadership, this project is run by elected board members and representatives who work hand-in-hand with dedicated staff to maintain the water infrastructure, ensure smooth operations, and uphold community rules. Our water is sourced from the pristine Kathita River, flowing straight from the majestic slopes of Mount Kenya, ensuring top-notch quality for every household. You’ll find our offices conveniently located next to St. Theresa Mission Hospital in Kiirua, where we remain committed to serving our people with integrity and purpose. At Kathita Kiirua Water Project, it’s more than just water, it’s about empowering the community with a resource that sustains life and strengthens unity.
